French Toast




Ingredients :

2 eggs
60 ml milk
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
a pinch of salt
Maple syrup
Two slices of bread
1 tablespoon unsalted butter



Instructions :
2 eggs
60 ml milk
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
a pinch of salt
Mix well
Two slices of bread
Cut peels (optional)
Medium-low heat
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
Pour the egg on the bread
Flip it over and cover it with the egg
Repeats
Pour in the rest of the egg
Soak the egg mixture with the bread
Put it in a frying pan
Shape the bread with spoons
flip up
Fry the sides
Add maple syrup
Finished!
