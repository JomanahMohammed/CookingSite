Egg Omelette




Ingredients :

3 eggs
salt
black pepper
Crushed red pepper
100 grams of green onions
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 tomato
Mozzarella cheese



Instructions :

3 eggs
salt
black pepper
Crushed red pepper
Mix well
100 grams of green onions
Cuts
Add it to the eggs
Mix well
Medium-low heat
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 tomato
slice
Place the tomato slices in the pan
Pour the eggs into the pan
Mozzarella cheese
Fry for 3-5 minutes
Finished!
