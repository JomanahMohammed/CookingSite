Pancakes




Ingredients :

2 eggs
2 cups (475ml) milk
2 1/4 cups (280g) self-raising flour



Instructions :

1. Whisk eggs and milk together.
2. Slowly add the self-raising flour whisking all the time. Mix to a smooth batter.
3. Heat a non-stick frying pan. Spray with a little cooking oil.
4. Spoon about Â¼ cup pancake mixture into the pan. Swirl mixture around to make pancake.
5. Cook pancake until it begins to bubble on top. Flip using a spatula and cook the other side until golden.
6. Serve with canned or fresh fruit, yoghurt and a little golden syrup.
